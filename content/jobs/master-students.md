## About the Role

The CNBD Lab is actively recruiting a motivated Master's student to join us in **Fall 2026** through the BME or related Engineering program at FIU. Students with backgrounds in biomedical engineering, neuroscience, electrical engineering, computer science, physics, psychology, or related fields are encouraged to apply. Applicants should have strong interest in neuroimaging, computation, and brain disorders research.


## Prerequisites and Requirements
Before applying, review the prerequisite information and complete the admission requirements provided by FIU BME. 


### Eligibility

Candidates for the Master's Program in the CNBD Lab must meet the following requirements:

- Completion of a **bachelor's degree** in biomedical engineering, cognitive neuroscience, electrical engineering, psychology, computer science, or a related field.
- A minimum cumulative undergraduate **GPA of 3.0** on a 4.0 scale. Graduate GPAs may also be considered if academic improvement is evident.
- Accepted for an MS program with a fellowship from FIU

### Suggested Coursework:

Applicants are encouraged to have completed coursework with **proficiency (B average or above)** in mathematics and science. Advanced courses in Signal processing, Computer science, Biology, chemistry, and physiology, are highly recommended.

### International applicants

International students are welcome to apply. See FIU’s [international applicant information](https://admissions.fiu.edu/how-to-apply/graduate-applicant/index.html) for details.


## Nice to Have

- Completion of a Bachelor’s degree in a related field.
- Bilingual: English and Spanish (if planning to conduct clinical research projects)
